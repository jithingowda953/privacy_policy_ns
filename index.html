Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Next Step, a job finding app developed and managed by Next Step company. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at: nextstepapp.1@gmail.com

3. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information:

  • Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email
  • Resume
  • Education
  • Work Experience
  • Location

Resumes and user data are securely stored in Firebase. We do not collect device data or IP addresses.

4. How We Use Your Information

Your personal data is used to:

  • Recommend suitable jobs
  • Allow one-click job applications
  • Send your resume to HR automatically when you apply for a job

HR personnel may view your application data to contact you.

5. Communication

We may send you emails and push notifications about job recommendations, updates, and other relevant information.

6. App Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Storage - for handling your resume
  • Notification - to alert you about job matches and important updates

7. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services such as Firebase and Google Sign-In. These services may collect data as per their privacy policies. Currently, we do not use in-app purchases or ads, but we may introduce them in the future.

8. User Control

You have the right to access, edit, or delete your personal data. These actions can be performed within the app.

9. Data Security

We use strong encryption and Firebase security rules to protect your data. Unauthorized users cannot access or modify your information.

10. Age Restrictions

This app is intended for users aged 18 and above. Users under 18 are not permitted to use this app.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified via email about significant changes.

12. Your Consent

By using our app, you consent to the collection and use of your information as outlined in this policy.
